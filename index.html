How Does Your Brain Respond to Transitions?

Transitions are the seams of adult life: switching tasks, moving between roles, the shift from morning to evening, the big life chapters that end and begin. For a lot of people, the friction is not in the work itself. It is in the moving between things.

This assessment looks at how your brain handles four kinds of transition and shows you where your friction lives. About 5 minutes. You get a personalised read at the end.
